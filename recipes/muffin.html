Moist and Fluffy Vanilla Cupcakes!

Description

Muffins are a delightful treat for breakfast or a snack. This recipe produces moist and delicious chocolate chip muffins that are sure to please your taste buds.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat the Oven:

    Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease or line a muffin tin with paper liners.

  2. Mix Dry Ingredients:

    In a bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.

  3. Add Wet Ingredients:

    In another bowl, beat the egg, then stir in the melted butter and milk.

  4. Combine Mixtures:

    Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.

  5. Add Chocolate Chips:

    Gently fold in the chocolate chips into the batter.

  6. Fill Muffin Cups:

    Divide the batter evenly among the muffin cups, filling each about two-thirds full.

  7. Bake:

    Bake in the preheated oven for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean.

  8. Cool:

    Allow the muffins to cool in the tin for a few minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  9. Serve:

    Serve and enjoy your delicious homemade chocolate chip muffins!
